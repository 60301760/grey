Our Story

From a passion for coffee to a beloved community cafe, here's a glimpse into our journey.

Grey Coffee was established in 2020 in Doha, Qatar as a specialty coffee roastery and cafe. We believe in creating calm, minimalistic spaces where customers can enjoy quality coffee and meaningful conversations.

Our beans are sourced from around the world and roasted in-house, ensuring each cup is as fresh and flavorful as possible. At Grey Coffee, we focus on delivering not just excellent coffee, but also a serene experience that invites you to slow down and savor the moment.
